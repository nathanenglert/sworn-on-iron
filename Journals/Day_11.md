# Day 11

The next day, we come across a river. There is a crossing, but it appears to be controlled by bandits. I throw a sheet over the cage, hoping not to attract attention to it. 

"Hey there, friend! Hold up a second. That will be two shillings to cross, please," says a [[Kabeera|quirky bandit]].

"What's the toll for?" I ask.

"Why, there's bandits in these parts! We volunteer ourselves to help guard travelers in these here parts."

"Thanks for the offer, but I should be OK." I touch the handle of my [[Axe]].
